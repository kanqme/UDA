Taking Action Against E-Waste

Electronic waste pollution represents one of the most significant environmental challenges of our time. Every year, millions of tons of electronic devices are discarded worldwide, creating a huge impact on the environment and human health..

The good news is that each of us can make a difference through conscious choices and concrete actions. On this page, you will discover how you can personally contribute to the fight against e-waste and promote a more sustainable lifecycle for electronic devices.

Creative Reuse

Giving New Life to Old Devicesi

Give your old devices a new use:

  • Turn an old smartphone into a security camera or baby monitor
  • Convert an obsolete laptop into a media center or home server
  • Use outdated tablets as digital photo frames or smart home controllers
  • Recycle electronic components for DIY and maker projects

Sharing and Donation

Put working devices back into circulation:

  • Donate working devices to schools, non-profit organizations, or libraries
  • Participate in technology exchange programs within your community
  • Sell or give away devices you no longer use on second-hand platforms
  • Look for technology donation programs for developing countries

Activism and Campaigning

Use your voice to demand structural changes::

  • Support petitions for stricter e-waste laws
  • Demand greater transparency from manufacturers about their supply chains
  • Promote the right to repair and regulations against planned obsolescence
  • Follow and support organizations working on environmental justice
  • Participate in public consultations on environmental issues

Supporting Innovation

Sustainable Design

  • Prefer companies that adopt eco-design principles
  • Support modular and easily repairable products
  • Look for devices made with recycled or biodegradable materials
  • Consider durability and upgradeability as key purchasing factors

Circular Economy
